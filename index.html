Interesting Facts About Camel

  • There are two types of camels: One humped or “dromedary” camels and two humped Bactrian camels.
  • Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.
  • Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants other animals can't eat.

Interesting Facts About Chameleon

  1. Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder.
  2. Changing skin colors is an imporstant part of communication among chameleons.
  3. Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches.

Interesting Facts About Flying Cicada

  • The loud whirring or buzzing sound you hear is an all-male cicada chorus.
  • They're true bugs (from the order Hemiptera).
  • Most have red-orange eyes.

Interesting Facts About Panda

  1. They have great camouflage for their environment.
  2. Their eyes are different to normal bears.
  3. But they do occasionally eat something other than bamboo.

Interesting Facts About Pigeon

  • Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals.
  • Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities.
  • Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities.

Interesting Facts About Teddy Bear

  1. A teddy bear is a stuffed toy in the form of a bear.
  2. The teddy was named after President Theodore Roosevelt, after he refused to shoot a bear during a 1902 hunting trip.
  3. Inspired by a cartoon featuring the event, shopkeeper Morris Michtom and his wife Rose made a stuffed bear with a sign 'Teddy's bear' and left it in their shop window.
